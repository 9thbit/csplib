The following give various basic CSP models of this problem:

  • A. M. Frisch, I. Miguel, T. Walsh. "Modelling a Steel Mill Slab Design Problem," Proceedings of the IJCAI-01 Workshop on Modelling and Solving Problems with Constraints, pages 39-45, 2001.
  • A. M. Frisch, I. Miguel, T. Walsh. "Symmetry and Implied Constraints in the Steel Mill Slab Design Problem," Proceedings of the CP'01 Workshop on Modelling and Problem Formulation (Formul'01), pages 8-15, 2001.
  • B. Hnich, Z. Kiziltan, I. Miguel, T. Walsh. "Hybrid Modelling for Robust Solving," Annals of Operations Research 130 (1-4), 19-39, 2003.
More recent results, and more sophisticated models can be found in the following:
  • A. Gargani, P. Refalo. An Efficient Model and Strategy for the Steel Mill Slab Design Problem. Proceedings of the 13th International Conference on Principles & Practice of Constraint Programming, 77-89, 2007.
  • P. van Hentenryck, L. Michel. The Steel Mill Slab Design Problem Revisited. Proceedings of the 5th International Conference on Integration of AI and OR Techniques in Constraint Programming for Combinatorial Optimization, 377-381, 2008.
  • P. Schaus, P. van Hentenryck, J-N. Monette, C. Coffrin, L. Michel, Y. Deville. Solving Steel Mill Slab Problems with Constraint-based Techniques: CP, LNS, and CBLS. Constraints 16(2), 125-147, 2011.
  • S. Heinz, T. Schlechte, R. Stephan, M. Winkler. Solving Steel Mill Slab Design Problems. Constraints 17(1), 39-50, 2012.
This paper describes the closely related variable-sized bin-packing with colour constraints problem, and approximation algorithms to solve it:
  • M. Dawande, J. Kalagnanam, J. Sethuraman. "Variable Sized Bin Packing With Color Constraints," IBM Research Report RC21350, 1998.
This paper describes a more general (and significantly more complex) version of the steel mill problem:
  • J. R. Kalagnanam, M. W. Dawande, M. Trumbo, H. S. Lee. "Inventory Matching Problems in the Steel Industry," IBM Research Report RC 21171, 1998.
